Pickleball Rules

Understand the basics before you step on the court

Game Overview

Pickleball is played either as doubles (two players per team) or singles. The objective is to score points by successfully hitting the ball over the net so the opponent cannot return it.

Serving Rules

  • Serve must be underhand.
  • Paddle must contact the ball below the waist.
  • Serve diagonally across the court.
  • Only one serve attempt is allowed.

Double Bounce Rule

After the serve, each team must let the ball bounce once before returning it. This means:

  • The receiving team must let the serve bounce.
  • The serving team must let the return bounce.
  • After that, volleys are allowed.

Non-Volley Zone (Kitchen)

Players cannot volley (hit the ball in the air) while standing in the non-volley zone, also known as the "kitchen."

  • You may enter the kitchen after the ball bounces.
  • You cannot step into the kitchen during a volley.

Scoring

  • Only the serving team can score points.
  • Games are typically played to 11 points.
  • A team must win by at least 2 points.

Quick Tips

  • Stay out of the kitchen on volleys
  • Control your serve placement
  • Communicate with your partner
  • Focus on consistency over power
